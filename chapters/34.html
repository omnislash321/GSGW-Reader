Sangun.

One of the most sinister and unsettling <Darkness Exploration Records>.

==========

While it appears similar to the Jangsan Tiger ghost story that was popular on the Korean internet, this ghost story has a stronger shamanic and bizarre color.

The paintings of ghosts, who claim to have been killed by a tiger referred to as Sangun, persistently change their appearance and pursue people, attempting to bewitch and drag away a Sacrificial Lamb.

It is one of the <Darkness Exploration Records> that went viral in the first half of 20XX, with a succession of reviews stating that just reading it makes one feel ill or uncomfortable.

==========

It was exactly as described.

Even I*, who can usually read text-based ghost stories without much trouble, had to stop several times while reading it because of the strangely lingering, unpleasant aftertaste.

The fact that similar ghost stories were popular and felt somewhat familiar also played a part.

Most people have probably heard of it at least once.

'The ghost story where a person eaten by a tiger becomes a ghost and visits those close to them...'

The so-called Changwi.

It’s a chilling tale where this ghost can only go to the afterlife if it finds another Sacrificial Lamb and passes its role onto them.

'Certainly, even just thinking about it is unpleasant.'

However, thinking calmly, there is a path to survival.

'A C-rank means it's a rank that still has a standard escape record.'

Even if it’s not easy, it doesn’t mean there’s no way to escape this Darkness.

The problem is... the method is quite bizarre, and because it forces one to make an unfair choice, the psychological pain and pressure can be suffocating.

It was the type of ghost story that enjoyed making the reader shudder with immersion.

Exploration Record #3

A man in his 50s who downloaded a landscape painting from the internet that was said to improve his career luck.

The painting in question was an ink wash painting in the late Joseon style, and he testified that the depiction of the mountains and fog in the dusk was strangely vivid even in low resolution.

After downloading it, he occasionally dreamed of wandering near the mountains in the painting, and each time, he strangely felt at peace and his concentration improved.

However, the dreams changed starting from the point he was suddenly promoted at work.

A voice calling out to him began to be heard from within the mountain fog.

The voice said that it is a scholar's duty to repay a favor once received, and the sound of someone walking heavily along a mountain path while crying sadly was heard, along with the sound of a beast's cry.

Strangely terrified, the man turned around and ran away.

Then, the sound of cackling laughter followed him, repeatedly saying, 'I found you, I found you.'

The dream of running away repeated every night.

As the man moved further away from the mountain in the landscape painting, the voice calling his name in all sorts of different voices also drew closer.

And he realized that in his dream, he had run all the way to the front door of his house.

Terrified, the man woke from his dream and immediately ran out of the house alone to move into a hotel. He stated it was to avoid the voice chasing him and to prevent his family from getting involved.

However, in the dream that night, the voice did not chase him.

Instead, he saw someone with a pale complexion slithering like a snake into his open front door with a giggle.

Only then did the man realize.

The voice hadn't been following him, it had been trying to find out where he lived to get inside.

Result: 3 family members missing, excluding the man himself. (6 months later, 3 unidentified bodies found at a nearby dam)

Later committed suicide while hospitalized for PTSD.

...It’s like this.

The worst part was being forced to imagine the psychological pain of the person caught in this ghost story.

Furthermore, there was something even more unsettling.

'……I've come too far.'

I* was already in a situation where I had been bewitched and dragged here by a Changwi.

The circumstance of having answered the calling voice several times.

The Talisman that had almost fallen off.

This was a description that would only appear at the end of a long exploration record, in a desperate situation just before everyone dies.

It meant I* was driven to the very edge.

'This is driving me crazy.'

I* brushed my hair back.

-Are you in trouble, Friend?

-Oh dear. Is it perhaps because of the Uninvited Guest who visited earlier? Uninvited guests are always unpleasant. Even more so if it's a place you don't want to be.

-So, I would like to give you a piece of advice. Would that be alright?

-I shall turn off Mr. Roe Deer’s light for you!

That peculiar ability that made my presence completely vanish, allowing me to move without being noticed by the Blue Mascot.

-You just have to quietly walk out that door and go home. There is no better way to avoid a tiring situation than this....

“…….”

There was a high probability that I* could deduce the correct escape route based on the <Darkness Exploration Record>.

So, if I* just didn't get caught by the Changwi, I* might really be able to get out of this ghost story easily!

'But...'

Isn't that for one person only?

'Could you perhaps do it for someone other than me?'

-For someone who isn't a Friend?

Braun’s voice distorted for a moment as if he had received an unpleasant proposal, but as a broadcaster, he still replied in a cheerful tone.

-Well. I might be able to do it, but if I were you, I wouldn't take such a risk!

-Mr. Roe Deer, everything has a set total amount, and doesn't it follow that the more you share, the less you have for yourself?

It seemed that if he used it for others as well, either the duration of his ability or its power would decrease.

'As expected, it's for one person if we want to play it safe.'

Still, to leave the kids behind, the conscience of a modern person who received public education…….

Capitalism holds me back.

Right.

Of course, if things get urgent, I'd have to quickly call Braun for an SOS and at least one of us would have to bail.

'Let's try as much as we can.'

-That's a pity! Understood.

Smile Stickers, Alice's buff and debuff foods, and even the Snow White Apple.

Although I* had just lost the fork of the Blood-Sucking Cutlery….

'It was originally a pair.'

Right, the knife remained.

I* pulled the remaining Blood-Sucking Cutlery from the pocket on my other thigh.

And I* was surprised.

'…It’s grown!'

The knife that revealed itself was no longer the size of dessert cutlery.

Delicate engravings were on the handle, and something like a very small red gemstone was elegantly set on it as a decoration.

One could tell at a glance that it had grown.

'Could it be that the blood the fork consumed was shared with this side?'

They are a pair, after all.

“…!! I-Is that a magic weapon for suppression from the security team?”

I* didn't answer. Let Manager Lee Byeong-jin misunderstand.

I* carefully wiped the knife, which was now about the size of a utility blade, on my clothes and put it in my jacket's front pocket, where it was easy to reach.

Please, let there be no reason to use it.

“Then I will search this house.”

“…?”

“I would like you to accompany me.”

“What!? A-Ah, no. I-I’ve already seen everything….”

“Since you've already seen it, I’m asking you to guide me.”

“Oh, o-okay... Yes.”

Manager Lee Byeong-jin seemed reluctant, but he followed me, sensing my mood.

That’s a relief. I* avoided the situation of him wandering alone, getting scared, and fainting.

“This is, uh... it was probably the kitchen. There was nothing to eat, though.”

The old house, rusted, dusty, and moldy, was filled with nothing but Talismans.

The kind of house you might see halfway up a mountain in the distance while driving on a provincial highway—old, abandoned, and no longer inhabited.

“Weren't there any inconveniences while staying here for a week?”

“That is... I wasn't thirsty, nor was I hungry…….”

“T-That ghost, every time the ghost visited every night, only then did I strangely feel like my energy was being drained….”

“Have you ever tried going out during the day?”

“…I have, but no matter how much I walked, only the forest appeared…… I kept coming back here because I was afraid the sun would set while I was outside.”

If so….

And I* stood at the dead end.

“It seems there might be an upper floor.”

Then, I* let it hold the remaining Blood-Sucking Cutlery, the knife, and threw it toward the empty space.

To be precise, toward the ceiling.

“Gah!”

Then, with a loud noise, the ceiling opened, and an old ladder came crashing down.

'Attic discovered.'

“M-My goodness! As expected of the security team…. The way you view space is indeed different from an ordinary person!”

“…….”

No, it isn't.

I* just saw it in the Exploration Record….

There is always an attic in an abandoned house.

At any rate, given the circumstances, it seemed certain that it led to an attic.

It looks like a place I* really don't want to go.

Creak.

'Impersonating the security team will remember this....'

As if I* had finished the search quickly because I* was competent.

That must have looked natural.

The Path to Survival
