“No, they said the case study was finished and they would escape sequentially in small groups—what on earth is happening?”

Wasn't this an encouraging result?

‘Wow~ incredible.’

Did this look like a situation for comfort?

‘Do those people have no sense of reality at all!’

The average survival rate for civilians in B-grade was in the 2% range.

The very act of deploying a large number of employees in a situation where there was no proper manual was a reckless gamble, yet they were expressing disappointment and self-praise at this staggering result?

Even when it came to expert consultation, they had only hastily adopted the opinion of one research team section chief who told them what they wanted to hear just to keep up appearances.

The reason was obvious.

‘Because it’s what they wanted to do!’

Thanks to that, he himself was happy to have obtained such interesting results to analyze, but he found it miraculous that this company functioned properly even with those higher-ups in the development department.

Although it’s common for family-owned companies to be a mess with no system when seen from the inside.
