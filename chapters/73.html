“Shall we go? This is fascinating.”

“Hey, we're in our high school forms right now, aren't we? Wow. What kind of dream is this?”

Certainly, it wouldn't feel very scary until now.

‘It probably doesn't feel real.’

After all, they had returned to their high school selves. Rejuvenation is usually a pleasant thing.

And while a school at night is a common setting for ghost stories, it can also feel romantic when there are many people and a bit of moonlight streaming in.

However….

“Uh, this student mannequin? Look at this?”

One person approached the motionless student in front of the back door.

Then pointed to the hand the student had extended toward the back.

“This, the hand gesture looks exactly like….”

[Students, we will now close the automatic curtains and turn off the lights for heating and safety.]

“Like they were trying to lock the back door.”

Thud.

The window curtains closed.

“Mommy!”

“Ah, you startled me!”

Just for a second.

Flicker.

After a slight delay, the lights in the classroom turned on.

“Ah, thank goodness.”

“I guess they were just trying to turn on the lights… Huh?”

Blood was flowing profusely over the ivory classroom floor.

“Uh… Uh-uh-uh-uh?”

Following the blood with my eyes,

One of the people was collapsed near the back door with their neck completely snapped backward.

An enormous amount of blood poured from the neck, twisted at a bizarre angle that a living human could never achieve, pooling between the floorboards.

Their eyes were wide open in shock, as if they hadn't expected it at all.

“Aaaaah!”

“Kkeu-heoo-eok!!”
