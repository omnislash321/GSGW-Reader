The candle that had been a drawing had become a reality.

‘…Wow.’

I forgot the situation for a moment and marveled at it.

As I recalled the brief explanation of what we were going to do with that candle, expectation swelled in my mind.

Whoosh.

The flame swept across the wick and…….

Nothing happened.

“…….”

Why… won’t it light?

Kim Soleum even showed off the crazy ability of summoning a third hand by flipping a coin into the air to try and light it, but that didn’t work either.

“…Ah.”

As if suddenly remembering something, Kim Soleum pulled out the instructions for the candle kit and quickly read through them.

“Is there a problem?”

“…….”

Then, with a somewhat dry face, he lowered the instructions.

“No. I’m not sure.”

Actually, I knew.

It was one of the phrases written under ‘Precautions for Handling and Use’ at the end of the notice written in messy, dark red letters.

The ‘Recommended Users.’

※This toy was created to relieve the fatigue of modern Earthlings.

For safety, aliens, extra-dimensionals, non-intelligent beings, gods, and any other non-human intelligent beings cannot use it. :)

Because Kim Soleum wasn’t actually a person of this place.

I had to hand it over to someone trustworthy.

Someone with a low probability of acting out, and someone with social skills and morality.

“Ms. Goral.”

“…!”

“Please.”

Go Yeongeun eventually swallowed her saliva and took the candle.

Then, she took the lighter and, with trembling hands, held it over the candle’s wick.

Whoosh.

It flared up.

“…!”

The keywords of the cards that had been drawn.

[Honor] [Interference] [Falsehood]

—Do you remember the cards we drew when making the candle earlier? They tell us what kind of ability the candle that will be made will have.

The explanation I heard from Kim Soleum came to mind, and at the same time, other phrases floated up from the candle.

The practitioner must lift this candlelight toward an honorable subject, rely on the power of interference, and reveal a false appearance.

A blessing is given to the practitioner holding the candle.

The Impersonator of Nobility.

Go Yeongeun held the candle high.

A round ball of fire rose from the candle like a lantern, hovered over her head, and then disappeared.

Then, as if under a small spotlight, her surroundings were softly illuminated.

‘Wow…….’

“The effect is exactly as expected.”

Kim Soleum, who was watching with a faint smile, nodded and said.

“Up to two people around Ms. Goral are affected by the candle’s area, so Goat and I will follow behind.”

“Yes.”

Kim Soleum whispered again.

“However, there is something you must be careful of. …Do not look back at us. We only receive the effect when we are hidden behind the one holding the candlelight.”

“……Yes.”

“And do not look too closely into the candle. …Your mind could be eroded by it.”

Go Yeongeun nodded slowly.

Tension tightened her shoulders, but Go Yeongeun somehow regained her composure.

Since this was her specialty.

“Since you said the exit is on the ground floor earlier… then that means we start from the 4th floor, the middle floor, right? That way, the number of possibilities is minimized.”

“Yes. Good.”

Their steps began.

Thinking about what was about to happen was enough to turn one’s spine cold, but Go Yeongeun bit her lip.

I must be the most faint-hearted one here, and yet I’m the one holding the candle.

‘I have to do my best.’

She had no idea that the biggest coward among them was right behind her!

the cold exchange between Kim Soleum and Baek Saheon made Go Yeongeun’s heart go cold as well.

“Hello.”

Creeeeeak.

The candle’s blessing was currently being successfully applied to her.

A blessing that allowed her to impersonate an honorable person.
