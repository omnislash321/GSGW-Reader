A ghost story set in a school.

In truth, it has been a long-standing tradition since ancient times.

Hundreds of teenagers have to spend all day in a single building; it would be more strange if they never took an interest in scary and bizarre rumors, mysterious rituals, or ghosts.

The Seven Wonders, Bunshinsaba….

Naturally, having attended such a school myself, I found them quite entertaining.

They are universal and popular horror stories, after all.

‘That’s why I gathered the courage to add an Exploration Record for the first time.’

Fortunately, the record wasn’t taken down due to a ‘Not Recommended’ rating terror, and using that as a catalyst, I even went as far as creating a ghost story myself….

But.

‘I’m… entering there?’

It was a strange feeling.

Naturally, the thought of entering a ghost story sent a chill down my spine first, but there was also a strange sort of nausea.

It wasn't like having an Educational Manual in front of me—no, it wasn't that….

Anticipation and fear?

‘I don’t know how to put it….’

Regardless, the Manual had already been distributed, and I had to at least ‘attempt’ to enter.

In that case.

-Are you going to perform a short ritual before falling asleep today? I’m curious if it’s Mr. Roe Deer’s own secret to falling into a deep sleep!

“It’s not that.”

I silently tidied my bed.

“I’m trying to have a ‘special dream’ the company told me about.”

-Ah, are you planning to go to that ‘high school’?

Yes.

‘Even if it’s not here, I have to enter a ghost story anyway.’

Since only a few people who meet the specific temporary conditions can enter at a time, it would be better to enter this one, as the company gives good Points for it.

‘Whew.’

Late night at the company housing.

I opened the problematic ‘Specific Tome’ I received from the company.

-On a night with a full moon, if you read the Specific Tome and fall asleep, you may wake up in the school at night.

It was exactly as Section Chief Lee Jaheon had explained.

The unique thing was that this was a text file.

In the Dark Shadow.txt

This file is said to be occasionally tucked into text file collections (which suspiciously ignore copyright) like ‘Masterpiece Horror Novel Collection of the 2000s’ downloaded from the internet.

The file size seemed large enough to be two books. However….

I clicked to open the file.

This all happened on our graduation day.

That single line was the end of the content.

And when I scrolled down further out of strangeness,

((Add))

The file cannot be opened.

It disappeared.

Baek Saheon was likely preparing to enter this ghost story as well. Since it was for all employees….

‘Maybe I’ll run into him inside.’

Well, the probability of Baek Saheon meeting the exact conditions to enter was low, though.

Every time this ghost story opened, the conditions for who could enter changed.

‘But I know the ‘Guaranteed Entry’ method that appeared in the mid-to-late part of the Exploration Records…….’

That’s why I had already prepared that method.

The important thing was that I didn’t know how much information had been released to the employees at this point.

‘Even if the Exploration Records haven’t accumulated much, the nature of this ghost story means other rumors might have circulated privately.’

Since all employees were preparing to enter, this point bothered me a bit.

Hmm.

‘Should I check?’

It was also the right time to keep him in check so he wouldn't do anything stupid.

I went to the front of Baek Saheon’s room and knocked politely.

Knock, knock.

“Wh-what!”

“I’m coming in.”

Creak.

“Isn’t it a social convention to wait for the answer ‘come in’ after knocking before entering? Eh?”

Knock, knock.

Knock, knock.

Knock, knock.

“The result is the same.”

“Anyway, why, I’m extremely curious as to why you suddenly felt the need to barge into someone else’s room for the first time in the middle of the night….”

Whether he reached a conclusion or not, he opened his mouth as if it were no big deal.

“……No, I heard a rumor at the company.”

A rumor.

Oh, he’s trying to lowball me naturally.

“You’re right, my hint doesn’t seem very valuable.”

“…Assistant Manager, if an employee living in the same housing extorts the salary and personal information of a poor new recruit through blackmail and threats, couldn't I at least request an interview with a superior for workplace harassment?”

“Do you… really believe this company would care about something like that?”
