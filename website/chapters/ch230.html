"I will take your silence as affirmation. Roe Deer-nim!"

'I was suspicious after all.'

It didn't matter if they looked at me suspiciously when I handed over the information and received the Wish Ticket.

I hadn't expected to meet like this again.

And I realized one thing.

'He doesn't know who I am.'

I came from a world where there were only the <Darkness Exploration Record> were wikis, a world without ghost stories.

As I rummaged through the Cheerful Research Institute in the basement of this Main Building, perhaps he had seen the experiment logs I saw, but he didn't seem to know the finer details.

That meant....

That the other side actually had information they wanted to know from me.

And I had no intention or need to answer.

     >Testimony of fear.

 > Unqualified as a Daydream employee.

"...It's okay. That only applies to regular employees."

Director Ho smiled benevolently again, like an innocent employee.

"I'm a contract worker, so I'm fine. ...You are too."

And calling out.

"Roe Deer-nim?"

"Please come with me, Roe Deer-nim."

[Let's think about the circus performers, friend. If you move your joints beyond their range of motion, you might put on a great performance, but there's always a risk that your bones might shatter.]

Right.

'Use it cautiously.'

And as for why I can do such things... calmly, quietly, deeply, until I die... No.

Let's avoid that for now. That also seems to affect my brain.

I need to organize what is necessary right now.

The director named Ho Yuwon.

And probably the counselor at the Fox Counseling Room.

I definitely received help from the counselor at the Fox Counseling Room. That teacher seemed like a good counselor back then... but ghost stories often have duality.

The current situation.

Ho Yuwon cannot easily impose restrictions on me.

"It seems difficult for one side to unilaterally suppress the other, Mr. Roe deer."

"Wouldn't we make a good pair to travel together? Because it's a situation where it's

[Oh, that's unfortunate, but that's the wrong answer! Mr. Roe deer always has a Good Friend by his side to help him!]

Unless the Host finds the other side more interesting and decides to help them.

My stomach feels like it's twisting. I only recently found out where my stomach is located, so it feels even more real... Ugh.
