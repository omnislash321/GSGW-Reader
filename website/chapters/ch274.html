"You see. I don't like stray dogs that touch and covet what belongs to others. Even more so if they're diseased."

Kim Soleum.

The moment the employment contract wavered at Director Cheong's fingertips.

The Security Chief, J3, regained consciousness two days later.

The recovery room of the Fox Counseling Room.

And next to him….

Question: Current status

I was sitting there.

"..."

The Security Chief stared at the sunlight coming through the curtains with a dazed look.

Then, as if to check something, he raised a hand, but soon lowered it, perhaps realizing the sunlight wasn't real.

And he looks at me, as if checking my condition.

I was perfectly fine, trailing Black Smoke as I gazed at him from behind my gas mask.

At that, the Security Chief's gaze seemed to blur with relief, but his next question comes out in a low, rough voice.

"...The Badger…?"

Next room

Long-term Recovery Room

(No Visitors)

Recovering

Location: Fox Counseling Room

His gaze, which had been scanning the surroundings, soon came to a halt. Once again, the old, empty, seemingly dead husk of a body lies quietly on the bed.

About the result of what his own Contamination had wrought.

"The Badger, his arms..."

Searching for regeneration method

"I see…."

"A phone... is there one...."

Question: Reason

"To contact the Security Team.... The Badger's… recovery costs… they can just put it on my tab…."

Because you're a lifetime employee anyway?

That the Security Chief had also become a lifetime employee through a process similar to mine.

This man's state.

'....'

In the end, I grabbed the memo pad and pen on the table and quickly wrote, showing it to him.

Don't worry.

I'm talking with Director Ho, so you don't have to worry about the costs.

The Security Chief didn't answer.

It's not that I trust Director Ho blindly, but I know what he needs right now, so I mean it will be fine.

I didn't answer.

Would it be okay to tell the Security Chief about Sekwang Special City right now?

The Security Chief has only just recovered.

Would telling him about a 'ghost story where no one can escape the lockdown' become another trigger?

Besides.

'The Nostalgia Candy only worked for a moment on the Security Chief, too.'

That thing,

It was a different version.

Indulge in an even more distant memory!

Nostalgia Can-dy, The Farther Flavor

<Fantasy Edition>

I had thought the candy felt strangely larger than the ones I used before, and after coming here and checking, I saw that the description on the wrapper was slightly different.

Director Ho is trying to move both of your affiliations to his project team entirely.

Probably at the sealed room beyond it.
