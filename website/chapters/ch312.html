It meant that just ending the subway stations one by one would be quite profitable.

Then it's between 100,000 and 200,000….

If I add the liquid I already had and the liquid I got from ending the Segwang High Ghost Story, it might really be 400,000 to 500,000.

There are many particularly dangerous places among the Segwang Metropolitan City subway stations. If I just keep dying in those places and going outside, the Dream Collector should fill up.

It's not for nothing that Daydream manages the cycles with intervals instead of putting the same person in the same ghost story every single day.

'Thanks, Braun.'

Looking at the Collector sloshing with A-grade essence, I steeled my resolve once more.

So, for now, I'll fill it up with the Dream Essence Liquid I've gathered and….

"No."

"How about putting that dream incubator back where it was."

That.

"Let's see, you said Jaegwan is in a place called the Train Shelter, right?"

"...Yes."

Guilt flared cold in my heart again, and my head cleared.

"I'll guide you."

We left the burnt-out hallway of the Cheerful Research Institute and headed for the platform.

"Roe Deer… ah."

When Ms. Go Young-eun saw Agent Choi, her eyes wavered, and she greeted him with an expression that was both pleased and awkward.

"Agent."

Agent Bronze smiled faintly.

He looked just like when he was alive.

Guilt.

"Why are you worried about something like that? With this ace Senior here…"

It was clearly a conversation about how to deal with Agent Bronze's absence.

And….

"…Is there anything you want to pass on to anyone?"

It seemed he was taking a last will and testament, just in case.

"Anxious?"

"…!"

"Noruya. We're seeing each other again sooner than I thought. You really ran off like some roe deer."

"I'm fine. It's just that I've made a few discoveries."

"Hmm…."

Eun Haje, who had been watching me quietly, said nothing and returned to Agent Bronze and Ms. Go Young-eun.

"We're going together, Grapes-ie!"

[Hmph. Still an excessive fellow, feigning friendliness.]

…If that's the case.

"By any chance, from now on during our explorations, if the Collector fills with liquid, could you… sell it to me?"

Morning Station (Scales Tribunal)
